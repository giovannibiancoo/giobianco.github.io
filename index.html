About Me

I am currently pursuing a MSc in Artificial Intelligence at Bocconi University in Milan, Italy after having graduated cum laude with a BSc in Economics and Computer Science.

My passion lies in Mathematics and Computer Science and I aim at a career in Machine Learning research, Software Engineering, and Data Science.

I also want to connect minds, not only layers. For this reason, I co-founded the first machine learning project-incubator at my university: Bocconi Students for Machine Learning (BSML).

Currently, I am working as a researcher at the Bocconi Institute for Data Science and Analytics in Machine Learning applied to Biology. In particular, I am developing BioFormer: a novel architecture for gene expression modelling. I am also exploring and empirically measuring scaling laws of machine learning models when applied to transcriptomics data.
